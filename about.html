Our Story

Masters in the art of patience and nature since 1995.

A Legacy of Living Art

In 1995, our founder embarked on a journey to bring the ancient and respected Japanese art of Bonsai to enthusiasts worldwide. What started as a small personal collection has grown into WithBonsai, a premier nursery dedicated to cultivating master-quality trees.

We believe that a Bonsai is not just a plant; it's a living sculpture, a meditation on time and patience, and a deep connection to the natural world. Each tree in our collection has been nurtured for decades with precise techniques, deep respect, and unwavering dedication.

Patience

True beauty cannot be rushed. Our master growers wait years, sometimes decades, to guide a tree to its ideal form.

Passion
